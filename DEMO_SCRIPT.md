## Demo Script (5-element)

### 1. Problem
Reddit has millions of communities but no sticky, competitive puzzle experience that uses the comment thread as the actual gameplay surface. People scroll passively; they don't come back daily with a competitive angle.

### 2. Before
Endless scrolling, passive consumption, no shared goal that brings people back tomorrow. Existing daily games are either trivia clichés or Wordle clones — nothing that feels genuinely fresh.

### 3. Trigger
Visit r/AgenticDaily. Today's Cascade puzzle is live, posted at 9am. The board shows a mechanic you've never seen combined with a theme pulled from today's Reddit headlines. Reply with your move.

### 4. Aha
Solve it in the comments. See your name on the leaderboard within minutes. Streak counter ticks up. Tomorrow's puzzle is seeded from today's Reddit themes, so it's always fresh and impossible to Google.

### 5. Close
Cascade is a social puzzle game where Reddit's biggest strength — the comment thread — becomes the gameplay itself. Agentic pipeline, Reddit-native retention, zero downloads.
